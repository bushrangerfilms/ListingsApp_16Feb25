# Getting started

AutoListing is a real estate platform that manages your property listings end-to-end — a listings manager, a CRM, a public-facing property website, a lead-capture system, billing, team management, and an automated social media engine all in one.

Internally, AutoListing is organised into two areas:

- **Socials** — everything to do with social media (scheduling, video generation, posting to Instagram/Facebook/TikTok/etc., lead-magnet quizzes, connected social accounts)
- The main area — everything else (listings, CRM, billing, team, settings, custom domains)

You switch between them from the sidebar. Same login, same account, same everything — just a clean visual separation.

## First-time setup checklist

When you first sign up, your dashboard shows an onboarding checklist. Complete each step to get the most out of AutoListing:

1. **Confirm your business details** — your business name, phone, and contact email. These appear on your public listings and emails.
2. **Set your location** — your office address and the areas you serve. Used for map displays and lead-matching.
3. **Add your branding** — upload your logo and pick your brand colors. Applied to videos, posts, lead-magnet pages, and emails.
4. **Choose your posting preferences** — decide whether social posts go out automatically or wait for your approval. You can change this later in Settings.
5. **Connect your social accounts** — link Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and any others you use. Without this, posts can't go out.
6. **Add your first listing** — create a property. AutoListing will start generating social content for it within a few minutes.

## What happens after you add a listing

Once a listing is created and you have at least one social account connected:

1. AutoListing generates videos and image posts for the property
2. A posting schedule is created — by default, new listings post twice a week for the first two weeks (the "launch phase"), then once a week ongoing
3. Posts go out automatically at the scheduled times (or wait in your approval queue if you've turned approval on)
4. You can drag posts in the weekly planner to reschedule, or click any post to edit the caption

## Where to find what

| You want to... | Where to go |
|---|---|
| Add or edit a listing | **Listings** in the sidebar |
| See your CRM and leads | **CRM** in the sidebar |
| View or change your scheduled social posts | **Socials → Scheduling** |
| Approve or reject pending posts | **Socials → Scheduling → Approval** tab |
| Connect a social account | **Socials → Social Accounts** |
| Configure lead magnets (quizzes, valuations) | **Socials → Lead Magnets** |
| Change your billing plan | **Billing** in the sidebar |
| Invite team members | **Team** in the sidebar |

If you ever can't find something, ask AL — the floating help button at the bottom-right of every page.
