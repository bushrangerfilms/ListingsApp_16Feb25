# Getting started

AutoListing is a real estate platform with two connected apps:

- **Listings** (app.autolisting.io) — where you manage your properties, leads, your CRM, and your team. This is where every property listing lives.
- **Socials** (socials.autolisting.io) — where AutoListing automatically generates videos and social media posts for your listings, schedules them, and posts to Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and Pinterest.

Both apps share the same login. Sign in once and you can switch between them.

## First-time setup checklist

When you first sign up, the Listings app shows an onboarding checklist on your dashboard. Complete each step to get the most out of AutoListing:

1. **Confirm your business details** — your business name, phone, and contact email. These appear on your public listings and emails.
2. **Set your location** — your office address and the areas you serve. Used for map displays and lead-matching.
3. **Add your branding** — upload your logo and pick your brand colors. Applied to videos, posts, lead-magnet pages, and emails.
4. **Choose your posting preferences** — decide whether posts go out automatically or wait for your approval. You can change this later in Settings.
5. **Connect your social accounts** — link Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and any others you use. Without this, posts can't go out.
6. **Add your first listing** — create a property in the Listings app. The Socials app will start generating content for it within a few minutes.

## What happens after you add a listing

Once a listing is created and you have at least one social account connected:

1. AutoListing generates videos and image posts for the property
2. A schedule is created — by default, new listings post twice a week for the first two weeks (the "launch phase"), then once a week ongoing
3. Posts go out automatically at the scheduled times (or wait in your approval queue if you've turned approval on)
4. You can drag posts in the weekly planner to reschedule, or click any post to edit the caption

## Where to find what

| You want to... | Open this app |
|---|---|
| Add or edit a listing | Listings (`app.autolisting.io`) |
| See your CRM and leads | Listings → CRM |
| View or change your scheduled posts | Socials (`socials.autolisting.io`) → Scheduling |
| Approve or reject pending posts | Socials → Scheduling → Approval tab |
| Connect a social account | Socials → Social Accounts |
| Configure lead magnets (quizzes, valuations) | Socials → Lead Magnets |
| Change your billing plan | Listings → Billing |
| Invite team members | Listings → Team |

If you ever can't find something, ask Al — the floating help button at the bottom-right of every page.
