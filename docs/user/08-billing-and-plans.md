# Billing and plan tiers

AutoListing offers seven plan tiers, billed in EUR (with GBP and USD also supported for those markets):

| Plan | Price | Active listings | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | €0 | 1 | Trying it out |
| **Essentials** | €40/week | 10 | Solo agents, light volume |
| **Growth** | €70/week | 25 | Solo agents, growing volume |
| **Professional** | €130/week | 100 | Single-branch agencies |
| **Multi-Branch S** | Contact us | 200 | Small multi-branch (2–3 branches) |
| **Multi-Branch M** | Contact us | 500 | Mid multi-branch (4–10 branches) |
| **Multi-Branch L** | Contact us | Unlimited | Large multi-branch (10+) |

Sold and Withdrawn listings don't count toward your limit.

## What's included on every plan

- Listings management + public property pages
- CRM with lead capture and pipeline
- Lead magnets (all 5 types)
- Email notifications and lead alerts
- Up to 6 markets supported (IE, GB, US, CA, AU, NZ)
- The Al chatbot assistant (with monthly query limits per tier)

## What changes between tiers

- **Listing limit** — see table above
- **Video styles** — Free has the basic style only; Essentials and above unlock advanced styles (VS2 motion clips, VS4 AI motion)
- **Custom domain** — paid plans only
- **Team members** — Free is solo; paid plans allow team invites
- **Branches** — Multi-Branch plans allow multiple branches with separate social accounts
- **Al chatbot quota** — Free has 20 messages/month, paid plans have higher quotas

## Upgrading or changing plans

1. Open **Listings → Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Pick the new plan and confirm

Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

## How billing works

- All plans bill **weekly** via Stripe
- You're charged on the same weekday each week
- Failed payment? Your plan switches to **past_due** and you get an email. After 3 failed attempts (over 7 days), the account is downgraded to Free.

## Custom currency

Your billing currency is set based on the country of your business address (or your IP location at signup):

- **IE**: EUR
- **GB**: GBP
- **US, CA, AU, NZ**: USD

To change currency, contact support — we can update it for you.

## Invoices and receipts

All invoices are sent to your billing email and available under **Billing → Invoices**. Click any invoice to download as PDF.

## Pause vs cancel

- **Pause** (paid plans only) — stops billing and stops new posts going out, but keeps your data and listings. Resume any time.
- **Cancel** — closes your account at the end of your current billing cycle. Listings stay accessible for 30 days then get archived. Reactivate any time within 90 days.

To pause or cancel, go to **Billing → Manage Plan**.

## Pilot customers

Some early pilot customers have a **billing override** that exempts them from plan limits. If you're one of those, you'll see "Pilot" or "Comp" badged on your plan. Don't worry about the limits in the table above — yours is set by us directly.
