# Adding and editing listings

All your properties live in the **Listings** app. Each listing automatically generates a public property page, gets included in your CRM lead-matching, and feeds the Socials app to create videos and posts.

## Add a new listing

1. Open the Listings app (`app.autolisting.io`)
2. Click **Listings** in the left sidebar
3. Click **+ Create Listing**
4. Fill in the property details:
   - **Address** — used for map display and area-based lead matching
   - **Price** — what gets shown publicly (you can hide this if needed)
   - **Property type** — house, apartment, commercial, etc. Affects which lead-magnet quizzes apply.
   - **Bedrooms / bathrooms / floor area**
   - **Description** — sell the property. Used on the public page and as input for AI-generated captions.
   - **Photos** — upload at least 8 high-quality photos. The first photo is the hero image.
5. Choose the **status** (see below)
6. Click **Save**

Within a few minutes, the Socials app picks up the listing and starts generating videos and scheduling posts.

## Listing statuses

Every listing has a status that drives how often it's posted on social media:

| Status | What it means | How often it posts |
|---|---|---|
| **New** | Just listed | 2× per week for first 2 weeks (launch phase), then 1× per week ongoing |
| **Published** | On the market, past launch phase | 1× per week ongoing |
| **For Sale** | Same as Published, alternate label | 1× per week ongoing |
| **Under Offer** | Offer received, sale pending | 1× per week ongoing |
| **Sale Agreed** | Price agreed, awaiting close | 1× per week for 2 weeks then stops |
| **Sold** | Sale complete | 1× per week for 1 week then stops |
| **Withdrawn / Off Market** | No longer listed | No posts |

The launch phase (extra posts in the first two weeks) only applies to listings that start as **New**. After 30 days, New listings auto-transition to Published.

## Editing a listing

Click any listing in the list to edit it. Changes save when you click **Update**.

When you change the **status**:

- The schedule for that listing is regenerated to match the new posting frequency
- Already-posted content stays on social media (it's not deleted)
- Future scheduled posts are reshuffled

When you change **photos**:

- New videos are queued to use the updated photos
- Already-generated videos use the photos they had when generated

## Bulk import

If you have many listings to add, contact support — we can do a bulk import from a spreadsheet or your existing CRM.

## Plan limits

The number of active listings you can have depends on your plan:

| Plan | Listing limit |
|---|---|
| Free | 1 active listing |
| Essentials | 10 active listings |
| Growth | 25 active listings |
| Professional | 100 active listings |
| Multi-Branch S | 200 active listings (multiple branches) |
| Multi-Branch M / L | 500 / unlimited |

Sold and Withdrawn listings don't count against your limit.
