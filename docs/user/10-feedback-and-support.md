# Sending feedback and getting support

There are three ways to get help in AutoListing.

## 1. Ask Al

Al is the floating help button at the bottom-right of every page. Click it to open the chat. Al knows about every feature in the app and can:

- Answer "how do I…" questions
- Explain why something is happening (e.g. why a post failed)
- Link you directly to the right page or setting
- Draft a feedback submission for you and send it on your behalf when you confirm

Al is read-only — Al can't change your settings or take actions in the app for you. But Al can guide you to do it yourself, or escalate to support if it can't help.

**Free plan:** 20 Al messages per month. **Paid plans:** higher quotas (200 / 750 / 3000 per month depending on tier).

## 2. Send Feedback button

The left sidebar has a **Send Feedback** button. Use this for:

- Feature ideas
- Bug reports
- Suggestions for improvements
- General feedback

Click the button, pick the type, write your message, optionally attach a screenshot, and click **Send Feedback**. The message goes directly to our team. Include your email so we can reply.

## 3. Al can submit feedback for you

If you tell Al about something that doesn't work, or a feature you wish existed, Al will offer to submit it as feedback on your behalf:

> *"That sounds like a feature request. I can submit it as feedback to the team. Here's the draft: [shows preview]. Want me to send it?"*

You'll always see what Al is about to submit and can edit or cancel before sending. Nothing goes out without your confirmation.

If Al can't answer your question after one clarifying question, Al will offer to escalate it as a bug report or general support question — same flow.

## What "feedback" actually does

Submitted feedback (whether via the Send Feedback button or via Al) goes to:

- An email to our team's support inbox
- Includes your business name, email, the page you were on, and the message
- We reply to your email address — usually within one business day

Bug reports and urgent issues get triaged first. Feature ideas are reviewed weekly.

## When NOT to use feedback

For these, use the right channel instead:

- **Billing question** — **Billing → Contact Support** link
- **Lost access to your account** — email support@autolisting.io directly
- **DNS / custom domain emergency** — see the Custom Domain doc, or escalate via Al
- **Security concern or data privacy issue** — email security@autolisting.io directly

For everything else, Al + the Send Feedback button cover it.
