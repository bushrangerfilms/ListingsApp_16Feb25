# Connecting your social accounts

To post content automatically, AutoListing needs to be connected to at least one social account. **Until you connect at least one account, no posts will be created or scheduled.**

## How to connect

1. Open the **Socials** app (`socials.autolisting.io`)
2. Click **Social Accounts** in the left sidebar
3. Click **Connect** next to the platform you want to add (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, or LinkedIn)
4. You'll be taken to that platform's login page — sign in and grant permission
5. You'll be returned to AutoListing with the account showing as connected

You can connect multiple accounts on the same platform — for example, two Instagram accounts for two different branches.

## Why connecting accounts matters

When you have **zero connected accounts**:

- New listings still create scheduling templates, but no actual posts are scheduled
- The dashboard shows an amber "Set up needed" warning
- Listings show as "Awaiting Setup" instead of "Active"

The moment you connect your first account, AutoListing automatically activates any pending listings and starts generating posts. You don't need to redo anything.

## Reconnecting an expired account

Social platforms sometimes expire their connections (Facebook, TikTok, Instagram do this every 60–90 days). When this happens:

- The account shows as **Reconnect needed** in the Social Accounts page
- You'll get an email reminder
- Posts to that platform will fail until reconnected

To reconnect: click **Reconnect** next to the platform — same flow as the original connection. You don't lose any history.

## Disconnecting

To disconnect: click the **⋯** menu next to the account → **Disconnect**. Posts already scheduled to that account will be cancelled. Posts that have already gone out remain on the platform — disconnecting doesn't delete them.

## Multi-branch organisations

If your account has multiple branches, each branch can have its own set of social accounts. When connecting, you'll be asked which branch the account belongs to. Posts for a listing always go out from that listing's branch's accounts.

## Troubleshooting

- **"Connect" button does nothing** — pop-ups may be blocked by your browser. Allow pop-ups for `socials.autolisting.io` and try again.
- **Connection succeeded but account doesn't appear** — refresh the page. If still missing, try disconnecting and reconnecting.
- **Posts are failing on a connected account** — check if it shows "Reconnect needed". Platforms often expire tokens silently.
